Curriculum Vitae

My research considers multiwavelength observations, from radio to gamma-rays, as well as optical spectroscopic observations to study the structure of the central engine and the high-energy emission mechanisms of blazars. Currently, I have co-authored 6 papers published in international refereed journals.

DEGREES

  • 2019-2022 (expected) - Ph.D. in Astrophysics - Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica (INAOE).
    Thesis: Study of the Multifrequency Activity of the Central Engine of Blazars
    Advisors: Vahram Chavushyan, Víctor Manuel Patiño Álvarez

  • 2015-2017 - M.Sc. in Physics - Instituto de Física, UASLP, San Luis Potosí, México.
